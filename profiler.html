Open Dataset Profiler

Drop in any tabular CSV — health records, hiring data, loan applications — and get an instant demographic representation audit: under-represented or missing subgroups, skewed age and sex distributions, geographic under-sampling, and intersectional gaps.

🔒 Your file never leaves your browser. All analysis runs locally on this page — nothing is uploaded.
